---
title: Supported Telephony
---

Configure UnleashX with your telephony providers to make and receive calls.

## Providers

- Twilio
- Plivo
- SIP/VoIP providers (via webhook/bridge)

## Requirements

- Verified phone numbers
- Regional compliance where applicable
- Callback URL configured in UnleashX dashboard

## Next steps

- Purchase phone numbers
- Make outgoing calls
- Receive incoming calls
