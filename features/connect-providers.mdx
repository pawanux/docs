---
title: Connect Providers
---

Connect external ASR, LLM, TTS, and telephony providers to UnleashX.

## Categories

- Transcribers (ASR)
- Large Language Models (LLMs)
- Synthesizers (Voices / TTS)
- Telephony (PSTN / SIP / CPaaS)

## Setup

1. Open UnleashX Dashboard → Providers
2. Select a provider and add credentials (keys/tokens)
3. Assign providers to agents or workflows

## Tips

- Use provider‑specific strengths per use‑case
- Enable fallbacks for reliability
- Monitor quality and cost metrics continuously
