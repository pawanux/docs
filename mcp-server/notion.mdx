> **In one line:** Connect Notion so your UnleashX agents can search pages, read content, and add new entries.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Authentication** | OAuth |
| **Setup time** | ~2 minutes |
| **Difficulty** | Easy |
| **Best for** | Teams who keep notes, docs, or databases in Notion and want agents to use them |

## 1. Overview

**What is Notion?**
Notion is a workspace for notes, documents, wikis, and databases.

**What can you do once it's connected?**
Search pages, read their content, and create new pages or database entries.

**Why connect it to UnleashX?**
Your agent can pull an answer from your knowledge base, log a call into a Notion database, or draft a page — without leaving the conversation.

## 2. What you'll need

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A Notion account.
- The pages or databases you want the agent to use (you'll share them during connect).

<Warning>
  Notion only exposes content you **explicitly share** with the connection. If the agent can't find a page, it probably hasn't been shared yet — see Step 4.
</Warning>

## 3. Get your credentials

Notion uses **OAuth**, so there's nothing to copy. You'll approve access and pick which pages to share in the next section.

<Info>
  Unlike most tools, Notion asks you to **select the specific pages** the connection can see. Choose carefully — this is your access control.
</Info>

## 4. Connect on UnleashX

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    In UnleashX, go to **Integrations** and search for **Notion**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">
    Click **Connect**, then **Authorize**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select pages to share">
    Sign in to Notion and choose the pages and databases UnleashX may access, then click **Allow access**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign to an agent">
    You'll return to UnleashX automatically. Assign Notion to the agent(s) that should use it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

✅ **You're connected** when the status shows a green **Connected** badge. The tools below are now available.

## 5. Available tools

| Tool | What it does | Changes data? |
|------|--------------|:---:|
| `search` | Find pages and databases you've shared | No |
| `get_page` | Read the content of a page | No |
| `query_database` | Read entries from a database | No |
| `create_page` | Add a new page or database entry | ✏️ Yes |

> ✏️ = creates or edits data

## 6. Example usage

**Example 1 — Answer from your wiki**

> You type: _"What's our refund policy?"_

What happens: UnleashX uses `search` to find the policy page, then `get_page` to read it back.

**Example 2 — Log a call**

> You type: _"Add a row to the Calls database: caller Acme, topic renewal, follow up Friday."_

What happens: UnleashX uses `create_page` to add the entry. You'll see a confirmation when it's done.

## 7. Permissions & data access

When you connect Notion, UnleashX can:

- Read the pages and databases you shared during connect.
- Create pages and entries within that shared content.

It **cannot**:

- Access pages you didn't share.
- Change workspace members or settings.

You can adjust which pages are shared, or disconnect entirely, from **Integrations → Notion → Disconnect**. Disconnecting immediately revokes UnleashX's access.

## 8. Troubleshooting

| Problem | What it means | How to fix it |
|---------|---------------|---------------|
| `401 Unauthorized` | The connection expired or was revoked | Reconnect from **Integrations → Notion** |
| Page not found | The page wasn't shared with the connection | Reconnect and share the page, or share it from Notion's **•••  → Connections** menu |
| `restricted` | The page is in a workspace you can't access | Confirm you're a member of that workspace |

Still stuck? See [Authentication basics](/mcp-server/authentication) or contact [cs@unleashx.ai](mailto:cs@unleashx.ai).

## 9. Frequently asked questions

**Is my Notion content stored by UnleashX?**
UnleashX reads pages only when a tool call needs them during a conversation; it does not copy your workspace.

**How do I give the agent access to a new page?**
Open the page in Notion, click **•••  → Connections**, and add the UnleashX connection — or reconnect and re-select pages.

**Can multiple team members connect Notion?**
Yes. Each person shares their own pages, so access reflects what each person chose to share.
