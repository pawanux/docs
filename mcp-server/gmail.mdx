> **In one line:** Connect Gmail so your UnleashX agents can read, search, and send email.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Authentication** | OAuth |
| **Setup time** | ~2 minutes |
| **Difficulty** | Easy |
| **Best for** | Teams who want agents to send follow-ups or look up email during a conversation |

## 1. Overview

**What is Gmail?**
Gmail is Google's email service for personal and business (Google Workspace) accounts.

**What can you do once it's connected?**
Search your inbox, read messages, and send new email or replies.

**Why connect it to UnleashX?**
Your agent can send a follow-up email after a call, find a past message for context, or confirm details — without you switching apps.

## 2. What you'll need

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A Gmail or Google Workspace account.
- For Workspace accounts: an admin who allows third-party apps, if your org restricts them.

<Warning>
  **On a managed Workspace account?** Some organizations block third-party apps by default. If authorization fails, ask your Google Workspace admin to allow UnleashX.
</Warning>

## 3. Get your credentials

Gmail uses **OAuth**, so there's nothing to copy. You'll approve access in the next section by signing in to Google directly.

<Info>
  You'll see Google's standard consent screen listing exactly what UnleashX can do. Review it before approving.
</Info>

## 4. Connect on UnleashX

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    In UnleashX, go to **Integrations** and search for **Gmail**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">
    Click **Connect**, then **Authorize**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in to Google">
    Choose your account, review the requested permissions, and click **Allow**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign to an agent">
    You'll return to UnleashX automatically. Assign Gmail to the agent(s) that should use it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

✅ **You're connected** when the status shows a green **Connected** badge. The tools below are now available.

### Permissions / scopes requested

| Permission | Why UnleashX needs it |
|------------|----------------------|
| `gmail.readonly` | To search and read your email |
| `gmail.send` | To send email on your behalf |

## 5. Available tools

| Tool | What it does | Changes data? |
|------|--------------|:---:|
| `search_emails` | Find messages by sender, subject, or text | No |
| `read_email` | Read a specific message | No |
| `send_email` | Send a new email or reply | ✏️ Yes |

> ✏️ = creates or edits data

## 6. Example usage

**Example 1 — Send a follow-up**

> You type: _"Email Priya a summary of today's call and ask for the contract by Friday."_

What happens: UnleashX uses `send_email` to compose and send it. You'll see a confirmation when it's done.

**Example 2 — Find context**

> You type: _"Find the last email from the Acme account about renewal."_

What happens: UnleashX uses `search_emails` to locate it and reads back the result.

## 7. Permissions & data access

When you connect Gmail, UnleashX can:

- Search and read messages in your mailbox.
- Send email as you.

It **cannot**:

- Delete email or change account settings.
- Access other Google services (Drive, Calendar) unless you connect them separately.

You can disconnect at any time from **Integrations → Gmail → Disconnect**. Disconnecting immediately revokes UnleashX's access.

## 8. Troubleshooting

| Problem | What it means | How to fix it |
|---------|---------------|---------------|
| `401 Unauthorized` | The connection expired or was revoked | Reconnect from **Integrations → Gmail** |
| `403 access_denied` | Your Workspace blocks the app | Ask your Google admin to allow UnleashX |
| Connected, but no results | The search matched nothing, or scope is read-only | Broaden the search; confirm the right account is connected |

Still stuck? See [Authentication basics](/mcp-server/authentication) or contact [cs@unleashx.ai](mailto:cs@unleashx.ai).

## 9. Frequently asked questions

**Is my email stored by UnleashX?**
UnleashX reads messages only when a tool call needs them during a conversation; it does not archive your mailbox.

**Can multiple team members connect their own Gmail?**
Yes. Each person authorizes separately, so email is always sent and read with that person's account.

**How do I revoke access?**
Disconnect in UnleashX, or remove UnleashX from your [Google account permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).
