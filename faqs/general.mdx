# General FAQs

## What is UnleashX?

UnleashX is an AI-powered platform that enables you to build human-like voice agents for phone conversations, chatbots for text interactions, and automated workflows. Our platform combines advanced AI technology with easy-to-use tools to help you create, deploy, and manage conversational AI at scale.

## How does UnleashX work?

1. **Create Your Agent**: Design your voice agent with custom prompts and settings
2. **Connect Phone Numbers**: Purchase or import phone numbers for your agents
3. **Deploy & Scale**: Make calls, receive conversations, and manage everything through our dashboard
4. **Analyze & Optimize**: Track performance and improve your agents with detailed analytics

## What's the difference between voice agents and chatbots?

- **Voice Agents**: Handle phone conversations with natural speech
- **Chatbots**: Manage text-based conversations via web chat, SMS, or messaging platforms

Both can be used together for a complete conversational AI solution.
